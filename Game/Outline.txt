Game : Program Outline
----------------------
Title: Should I marry him?
    
Summary : A girl is trying to decided if she should marry a boy. this programs helps decided when is the right time with a calculator.  



Gameworld
----------


Characters:                       Attributes 
The Girl (You) :            name, age, house skills, adult skills, readyness

The potiential Mate :       age, maturity level, his family, readyness

Girl Freinds :              percent that is married already(best friend), peer presure

Parents :                   tradition pressure, readyness






Win  /  Loose
-------------

- Win: You ask him to marry when everyone is ready Or you don't ask because he is NOT ready.

- Loose: asking him to marry when not everyone is ready. Not asking when everyone IS ready.
